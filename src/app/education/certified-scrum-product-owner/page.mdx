## Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO)

The CSPO certification from Scrum Alliance validates the ability to serve as an effective Product Owner in Scrum teams.

### Key Competencies

- Product backlog creation, refinement, and prioritization
- Stakeholder management and communication
- Maximizing the value delivered by the Scrum team
- User story writing and acceptance criteria definition
- Sprint planning and review facilitation
